Mathematical Formulas

Effective Dielectric Constant (Both Slot Line & CPS)

\[ \epsilon_{\mathrm{eff}} = \frac{\epsilon_r+1}{2} + \left(\epsilon_r-\frac{\epsilon_r+1}{2}\right)\left(1-e^{-f/f_p}\right) \] where \( f_p=\frac{c}{4h_m\sqrt{\epsilon_r}} \) and \( h_m = h/1000 \) (m)

Slot Line Characteristic Impedance

\[ Z_{0,\mathrm{slot}}=\frac{60}{\sqrt{\epsilon_{\mathrm{eff}}}}\ln\!\left(\frac{1.9\,h_m}{s}\right) \]

CPS Characteristic Impedance

\[ Z_{0,\mathrm{CPS}}=\frac{30\pi}{\sqrt{\epsilon_{\mathrm{eff}}}}\frac{1}{\frac{W}{S}+1.393+0.667\ln\Bigl(\frac{W}{S}+1.444\Bigr)} \]
